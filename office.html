Office Web Apps Viewer

Only files under 25 MB can be previewed with the Google Drive viewer.

  • Office Viewer Link: http://view.officeapps.live.com/op/view.aspx?src=[OFFICE_FILE_URL]
  • Office Viewer Embedded Link: https://view.officeapps.live.com/op/embed.aspx?src=[OFFICE_FILE_URL]

Office Viewer only previews office document types, listed below:

  1. Microsoft Word (.DOC and .DOCX)
  2. Microsoft Excel (.XLS and .XLSX)
  3. Microsoft PowerPoint (.PPT and .PPTX)
